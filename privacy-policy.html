3. Information We Collect

Summary: Velmora does not collect personal or sensitive user data and does not send data to our servers or third parties.

3.1 Personal Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store on our servers, or share any of the following:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or account credentials
  • Location data (precise or approximate)
  • Contacts, photos, files, SMS, or call logs
  • Advertising ID, device identifiers for tracking, or analytics data sent to us
  • Payment or financial information (the App contains no in-app purchases in this version)
  • Health, biometric, or other sensitive categories of personal data

3.2 Local Data Stored on Your Device Only

To provide gameplay features, the App stores the following information locally on your device using Android DataStore (velmora_play_prefs). This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us:

Data Type Purpose Shared?
High score (best score) Display and track your personal best No
App settings (sound, vibration, dark mode) Remember your preferences No
Unlocked achievement IDs Local achievement progress No
Golden crop harvest count Local achievement tracking No

This local data is not linked to your identity and is not accessible to us.

4. How We Use Information

Because we do not collect personal information, we do not use personal data for advertising, profiling, marketing, or analytics.

Local device data listed in Section 3.2 is used solely to operate game features (scores, settings, and achievements) within the App on your device.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

We do not:

  • Share data with advertisers or ad networks
  • Use analytics or crash-reporting services that collect user data
  • Integrate social login or third-party identity providers
  • Transfer data across borders to our servers (no such servers are used)

We may disclose information only if required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request. Given that we do not collect personal data, such disclosure would generally not apply to App usage data held by us.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Local data remains on your device until you delete it. You can remove all App data by:

  • Uninstalling Velmora from your device, or
  • Clearing app storage via Android Settings → Apps → Velmora → Storage → Clear data

We do not retain copies of your local game data on our servers because we never receive it.

7. Data Security

Local data is protected by your device’s operating system and app sandbox. We do not transmit this data over the network. While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, limiting data to on-device storage reduces exposure compared to cloud-based collection.

Android backup (allowBackup) may allow local app data to be included in device backups managed by Google or your device manufacturer, according to your system backup settings. We do not control third-party backup services.
